Harness Design Dominates Model Choice Within a Tier

J. Benabderrazak, A. Kim, R. Flores
Submitted 2026-03-30; last revised 2026-04-18
Abstract. We study five agent harnesses over a single frozen model and find a 3.2x spread in task success rate and 7.4x spread in cost on a 40-task HTML extraction benchmark.
