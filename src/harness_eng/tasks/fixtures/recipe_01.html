Weeknight Carbonara

by Rina Patel · Feb 11, 2026
Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Serves: 4 Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 150 g guanciale
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 50 g pecorino
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Crisp guanciale.
  3. Temper eggs and toss.
